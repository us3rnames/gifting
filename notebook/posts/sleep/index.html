connections

April 8, 2025, 5:02 am

sometimes, i'm afraid that i haven't made many connections my sophomore year of college. however, why not appreciate the connections i already have?

the existence of social media has only exacerbated the existing problem of the quantification of friendship. the best way to describe the feeling is, catching yourself worrying about the amount of friends you have compared to others, only to feel guilty because you worry that you're subconsciously turning your friends into a commodity.

i want to take this post to appreciate my friends. to the people from who i have infinite lessons to learn: thank you !
